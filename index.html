Tempus​Ware

Began creating content in 2014, starting with Minecraft Doctor Who animations and title sequences,

Created animations using audio excerpts from Yellowbunbunz's gameplay videos,

Directed a machinima comedy web-series from 2017 to 2018 called #WaddleOnRewritten.

#WaddleOn​Rewritten

Premiered on February 17, 2017 on TempusWare's channel before moving to its own channel after the first episode,

Run by community members of Club Penguin who dedicated their free time to act, write and edit for the project.

- 18 Episodes

- 128 Skits

Klode Through Time

Developed from August 17, 2017 as a collaboration between artists of the Club Penguin Rewritten community,

Abandoned on January 24, 2018 after some members dropped out of the project,

Reconstructed and released on July 11, 2019 using completed panels and rewritten text to finish the story.
